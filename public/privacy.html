Privacy Policy

Effective: April 28, 2026 · XaePay (operated by Chukie Okoli)

This policy describes the personal data we collect when you use XaePay, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it.

What we collect

  • Account information you give us when signing up or onboarding: name, email, phone, business name, business registration details (CAC, BDC license, etc.), and the role you select.
  • KYC documents you upload or that an Exchange Operator captures on your behalf: government-issued ID, BVN, proof of address, beneficial-ownership disclosures.
  • Transaction details necessary to facilitate trade payments: amount, currency, beneficiary, supplier invoices, customer rate, settlement timing, status events.
  • Communication metadata when you message us via WhatsApp or email: phone number, message content related to a quote or transaction, timestamps.
  • Operational logs: IP address, browser, device, page navigation. Used for security, debugging, and fraud prevention.

How we use it

  • To provide and operate the XaePay service.
  • To run compliance checks (sanctions screening, KYC verification, payer-name match, invoice integrity) required by our rail partners and applicable regulations.
  • To communicate with you about quotes, approvals, and transaction status — primarily via WhatsApp.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized use.
  • To improve the product based on aggregated, non-identifying usage data.

Who we share with

  • CBN-licensed BDC and Payment Agent partners who actually execute payments on your behalf.
  • Rail providers (Triple-A, Cedar Money, and similar licensed payment infrastructure) who move funds across borders.
  • Regulators and law enforcement when required by law — including the Central Bank of Nigeria, EFCC, SCUML, and equivalent authorities in jurisdictions we operate in.
  • Service providers who help us operate (e.g., Supabase for database, Vercel for hosting, Formspree for waitlist email, Meta for WhatsApp Business messaging). These vendors process data only on our instructions and under appropriate data-processing agreements.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Where data lives

Our database is hosted in EU regions (Frankfurt / London) by Supabase. Some processing happens in the US (Vercel edge network, Meta WhatsApp). KYC documents are stored encrypted in EU regions; transaction records are retained per applicable record-keeping requirements (typically 5–7 years under Nigerian financial regulations).

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion (subject to regulatory retention rules — we cannot delete records we are legally required to keep).
  • Export your transaction history.
  • Withdraw consent to non-essential processing at any time.

Email okoli.chukie@gmail.com with subject "Privacy request" to exercise any of the above. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

XaePay is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. KYC documents are stored privately with row-level security policies enforcing per-operator access. We do not custody customer funds.

Changes to this policy

We will post material changes on this page and notify users of significant changes via email or WhatsApp. The "Effective" date above reflects the current version.
