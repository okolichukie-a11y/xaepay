Data deletion

Effective: April 28, 2026 · XaePay

You have the right to request deletion of personal data we hold about you. This page explains how to make that request and what happens after.

How to request deletion

  1. Email okoli.chukie@gmail.com with the subject line "Data deletion request".
  2. Include the email address and/or phone number you used with XaePay so we can locate your records.
  3. Briefly describe what you'd like deleted — your full account, specific transactions, KYC documents, or all of the above.

You can also message +234 814 957 1908 on WhatsApp with the same details.

What we delete

  • Your account profile (name, email, phone, business info you provided).
  • KYC documents you uploaded (ID, BVN slip, proof of address).
  • Communication history with us (WhatsApp messages, support emails).
  • Operational logs that contain identifying information.

What we keep

Some records must be retained to comply with Nigerian financial regulations and our partner-rail requirements:

  • Transaction records for 5–7 years after the last transaction (CBN, EFCC, AML retention rules).
  • Sanctions screening logs to demonstrate compliance during regulator audits.
  • Aggregate, non-identifying analytics (e.g. total transactions per corridor) — these contain no personal data.

After the legally required retention period, the remaining records are also purged.

How long deletion takes

  • We confirm receipt of your request within 72 hours.
  • Account profile + KYC document deletion is completed within 30 days.
  • You'll receive an email/WhatsApp confirmation once deletion is complete, listing what was removed and what was retained (with the legal basis).

Effects of deletion

  • You will be unable to sign in to your XaePay account.
  • Open transactions or quotes tied to your account will be flagged as "customer record deleted" but the operator (BDC) you worked with retains their copy of the transaction record.
  • You may lose access to documents (e.g. MT103 references) — download anything you need first.

Disputes

If you disagree with our retention decisions or believe data was not properly deleted, you can:

  • Reply to our confirmation email asking for review.
  • Contact the Nigerian Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at nitda.gov.ng/ndpr.
